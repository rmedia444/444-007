Conference and Exhibition Services

We offer comprehensive conference and exhibition services, ensuring the seamless planning, organization, and execution of your events. Whether you are hosting a conference, seminar, trade show, or exhibition, our team is dedicated to managing every detail, from venue selection and logistics coordination to audio-visual setups and participant registration. Our on-site support ensures your event runs smoothly and effectively, providing your attendees with a seamless and enriching experience. With our expert consulting and planning, your business can maximize the impact of its events, fostering networking opportunities, knowledge sharing, and brand promotion.

General Cargo Warehousing and Logistics Services

Our general cargo warehousing services are designed to provide secure storage and efficient handling of a wide range of goods, excluding hazardous chemicals and other items requiring special licensing. We offer state-of-the-art storage facilities, comprehensive inventory management, and efficient loading and unloading services. Our goal is to streamline your logistics process, ensuring your goods are stored safely and transported efficiently. In addition to warehousing, we provide corporate management consulting to help you optimize your logistics and supply chain operations. Our labor services support your warehousing activities, ensuring you have the workforce needed to maintain smooth operations.

Human Resources and Labor Services

We specialize in human resources and labor services to support the workforce needs of your organization. Our human resources services offer expert guidance on employment practices, workforce planning, and employee relations, excluding intermediary activities and labor dispatch services. Additionally, our enterprise management consulting services help you improve operational efficiency and organizational structure. For your specific labor needs, we provide labor dispatch services, offering temporary or permanent staff to ensure you have the necessary human resources to achieve your business objectives. With our support, you can focus on your core business while we take care of your staffing requirements.
